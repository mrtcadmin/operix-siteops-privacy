Operix SiteOps Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Operix SiteOps is an enterprise construction workforce and site operations management application developed by MARCO to support construction project execution, workforce management, QR attendance, and daily site operations.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the Operix SiteOps mobile application.

Information We Collect

Employee Information

  • Employee ID
  • Employee name
  • Assigned responsibilities
  • Project assignments

This information is used only to authenticate authorized users and provide access to approved project functions.

Authentication Information

  • Employee ID
  • Password

Passwords are transmitted through encrypted HTTPS connections and are not displayed or shared with other users.

Camera Access

The application uses camera permission only for QR code scanning related to workforce attendance, task assignment, and construction site operational workflows.

The application does not capture or store photographs or videos.

Information We Do Not Collect

The current version of Operix SiteOps does not collect:

  • GPS location
  • Background location
  • Personal photos
  • Personal files
  • Contacts
  • SMS
  • Call logs
  • Microphone recordings
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics data

How Information Is Used

Information is used only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Workforce authentication
  • Attendance management
  • QR verification
  • Task assignment
  • Project management
  • Daily construction operations
  • Security and audit logging

Data Storage

Operational data is securely stored within MARCO's enterprise systems. Communication between the mobile application and backend services is encrypted using HTTPS.

Data Sharing

MARCO does not sell personal information. Information is only shared with authorized personnel within the organization for operational and administrative purposes.

Data Security

MARCO applies administrative and technical measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

User Rights

Authorized users may contact their system administrator regarding account information, access requests, correction requests, or account deactivation.

Children's Privacy

Operix SiteOps is an enterprise application intended for authorized company users only. It is not directed toward children under 18 years of age.

Future Location-Based Features

Future versions of Operix SiteOps may include location-based features such as geofencing. If location features are introduced, this Privacy Policy and the Google Play Data Safety declaration will be updated before release.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when new features are introduced or legal requirements change. The latest version will always be available on this page.
